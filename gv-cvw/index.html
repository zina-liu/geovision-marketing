Product core

Two Viewer Roles for Verified Event Response

With Operator and Supervisor Viewers, verification and review are handled by two dedicated roles, helping teams manage high event volumes and move alerts efficiently from verification to decision.

Operator Viewer

Verify the event before it becomes a response.

Operators review live event evidence, check the snapshot and event details, then close routine alerts or escalate verified incidents for supervisor handling.

Event review Video snapshot Decision making Escalation action

Supervisor Viewer

Turn verified reports into coordinated follow-up.

Supervisors review verified reports, confirm event context, and notify customer groups or security teams with evidence-based actions.
