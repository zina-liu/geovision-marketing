The challenge

Traditional Monitoring Often Stops at the Alert

Cameras record. Sensors trigger. Alerts arrive. But after an event appears, teams still need to verify what happened, decide who should respond, notify the right people, and keep a complete record.

Passive Recording

Video is available, but response often happens after the fact.

Disconnected Alerts

Sensor events are difficult to verify without related video.

Fragmented Follow-Up

Reports, notifications, and case records are handled across separate tools.

Product core

Two Viewer Roles for Verified Event Response

Operator Viewer and Supervisor Viewer separate first-line verification from supervisory review, so every event can move from alert to decision with the right level of control.

Operator Viewer

Verify the event before it becomes a response.

Operators review live event evidence, check the snapshot and event details, then close routine alerts or escalate verified incidents for supervisor handling.
